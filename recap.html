Up at the ski area

Family Adventure Park

A whole mountain of extra fun a short drive from the condos — great for the kids and the daredevils.

🎢 Mountain coaster (~25 mph) 🧗 Climbing wall + Quick Jump 🛷 Summer tubing 🚡 Zip lines & canopy tour 💎 Gem mining ⛳ Mini golf 🧒 Kids' Adventure Course (12 & under) 🚠 Scenic chairlift

Summer tubing runs about $10 for 2 rides or $14 for 4. Most attractions are priced individually, and a waiver is required (a parent signs for anyone under 18).

Good to know: it's at 3827 Massanutten Dr (up in the ski area), usually open about 10am–4pm, and appears to be closed Thursdays — so plan it for Friday, Saturday or Sunday. It's weather-dependent, so check before you head up.

🔗 Adventure Park info 🕑 Hours & rates 📞 540-289-4022
